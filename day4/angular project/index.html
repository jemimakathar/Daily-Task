___About Us____



Welcome to Our Restaurant

At The Coastal Bistro, we bring the freshest flavors of the ocean right to your table. Our cozy, beach-inspired atmosphere combined with our commitment to high-quality, locally-sourced ingredients creates a dining experience you won’t forget. Whether you’re joining us for a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or a casual meal with friends, our menu offers something for everyone.

Our Menu

Dive into our extensive menu featuring everything from succulent seafood dishes and perfectly grilled steaks to delightful vegetarian options. Highlights include our famous Lobster Roll, Pan-Seared Scallops, and our house-made Key Lime Pie. Don’t miss our daily specials that highlight the best seasonal ingredients.

Events and Catering:

Planning a special event? Our private dining area is perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, and corporate gatherings. We also offer catering services for off-site events, bringing our delicious dishes directly to you.
